🌸 Our Story

A Labor of Love,
Beautifully Curated

Velvet & Bloom is a woman-owned, small business based in Virginia, USA. We are excited to share our love of gift giving with personalized touches and beautiful aesthetics as we help you take care of your loved ones — no matter the occasion.

Our Mission

Helping You Celebrate With Intention

At Velvet & Bloom, we believe that every gift should tell a story. Whether it's a birthday surprise, a wedding favor, or a random act of kindness, we curate each package with intention, care, and a touch of luxury.

Our Curator-in-Chief personally selects every item that goes into our mystery scoops and custom curations, ensuring that each piece is a reflection of beauty, thoughtfulness, and quality.

Our Journey

From Dream to Reality

2024

The Idea Blooms

Velvet & Bloom was conceived from a passion for gift giving and a desire to bring curated luxury to everyday celebrations. Social media accounts launched; community building began.

NOV

Official Launch

On November 16th, 2024, Velvet & Bloom officially opened for business — just in time for the giving season. Mystery scoops and custom curations became available for ordering.

2026

Growing & Thriving

Expanding our collections with event gifting packages, order enhancements, and the beloved Velvet & Luxe line. Introductory pricing still available as we continue to grow with our community.
