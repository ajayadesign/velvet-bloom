❓ Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our products, ordering, and shipping.

🎁 Products & Collections

What is a Velvet Mystery Scoop?

A Velvet Mystery Scoop is a curated surprise gift package available in three tiers: Tier 1 ($35), Tier 2 ($55), and Tier 3 ($75) — all at introductory sale prices. Each scoop contains hand-selected items chosen by our Curator-in-Chief. The contents are a mystery — that's what makes them so exciting! Think stationery, self-care items, bookish goods, and beautiful accessories.

What's the difference between the scoop tiers?

Each tier offers an increasing level of luxury and quantity. Tier 1 is our starter experience with a curated selection of delightful items. Tier 2 is an elevated experience with premium products and more pieces. Tier 3 is the ultimate luxury scoop with our most indulgent curations and the most items.

How do Custom Curations work?

Tell us about your recipient's interests, preferences, and the occasion. Our Curator-in-Chief will personally select and arrange items into a beautiful gift box tailored specifically for them. Our Velvet & Luxe line starts at $170 (introductory pricing) for our most premium custom experience.

What are Order Enhancements?

Enhancements are add-ons you can include with any order — custom handwritten note cards, premium gift wrap upgrades, dried flower toppers, photo inserts, and more. They add an extra personal touch to your gift.

🛒 Ordering & Shipping

Do you ship nationwide?

Yes! We ship throughout the United States from our Virginia base. Shipping times and costs vary by location. Follow us on social media for restock announcements and availability updates.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover, Apple Pay, Google Pay, PayPal, and Shop Pay. All transactions are securely processed through Shopify.

Can I send a gift directly to someone else?

Absolutely! Simply enter the recipient's shipping address at checkout. You can also add a custom note card through our Order Enhancements to include a personal message.

🎉 Events & Bulk Orders

Can I order for corporate events or weddings?

Absolutely! Our Event Gifting Signature Packages are designed for weddings, corporate events, baby showers, and more. Contact us for bulk pricing, customization options, and lead time requirements.

How far in advance should I order for an event?

We recommend reaching out at least 4-6 weeks before your event for custom packages. This gives us time to source items, curate each package, and ensure timely delivery. Rush orders may be available — contact us to discuss.

Still Have Questions?

We're happy to help! Reach out and we'll get back to you within 24-48 hours.
